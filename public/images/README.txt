# Author Image Setup

Please download an author image and save it as:
/public/images/author.jpg

Recommended:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 400x400px or larger (square)
- Professional headshot or portrait
- Cameroonian author/entrepreneur image

You can:
1. Use a free stock photo from Unsplash/Pexels
2. Use your own professional photo
3. Use an AI-generated professional portrait

Once the image is in place, it will automatically appear on the homepage and author section.
